## Problem Statement:
  #### Description of healthcare problem being addressed
  Our protoype aims to assist in the diagnoses and monitoring of sleep deprivation, a human health condition. While driving, sleep deprived individuals experience a symptom of sleep deprivation called fatigue which induces microsleep. Microsleep is a brief period of sleep lasting around 30 seconds causing around 600 recent deaths [1] and 50,000 injuries [2]. 
  Our prototype aims to detect a sleep deprived individual’s increased blinking rate(an indicator of sleep deprivation [3]) moments before micro sleep occurs.

  
  #### Target user population
  Our low cost prototype using commercially available off the shelf products is intended to be used for all individuals around the globe, in an accessible low cost manner.
  
  #### Importance and relevance of the problem
  This is a relevant issue causing the death of roughly 2 ($600 / 365$) individuals per year

  #### Background & research
  Our team conducted general research including reading various websites and articles, and more narrowed research using source [3] to curate our software solution to accuratly differentiate between an
  alert and fatigued state.
